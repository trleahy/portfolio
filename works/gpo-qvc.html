Graphics & Playout Operator

QVC UK

Dec. 2022 - Current

As a Graphics & Playout Operator, I play a crucial role in delivering captivating visual content for live broadcasts.

My responsibilities include operating graphics systems, creating engaging visual elements using software like Adobe Photoshop, and ensuring seamless playout of graphics during live shows. I am also responsible for asset management, monitoring and playout, providing stills, TVs and other assets for use by the director. I collaborate closely with the production team to produce high-quality, on-brand graphics that enhance the viewer experience and contribute to the overall success of the broadcast.
